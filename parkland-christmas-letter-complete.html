A Message from our Association President, Roger Hebblethwaite

As Christmas draws near and time with family and friends, I want to extend my best wishes to you and my thanks to all the members of our local Conservative family for your support over the last year. Whether by volunteering during the Election in April, attending our AGM in October, or donating generously throughout the year, your support of our MP, Dane Lloyd, and our Association means everything to the strength of our movement.

Looking ahead

January will bring our CPC National Convention to Calgary, and we will be sending a delegation to participate. This will be an opportunity for developing policy, connecting with Conservatives from across Canada and working together to strengthen our Party and our message to Canadians. A significant goal of our Association for 2026 is to grow our communication and connection with you and all our grassroots members. You can look forward to hearing more about that in the New Year.
